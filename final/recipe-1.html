Basic Sourdough Bread Recipe

This is a very easy recipe, but it takes about 18-20 hours to prepare...most of that time being inactive time for the baker--while they wait for fermentation and rising.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups unbleached bread flour (about 480 grams or 17 ounces)
  • 1½ teasoons table salt
  • ¼ cup vigorous* sourdough starter
  • 1½ cups lukewarm water

Directions

Before bed, the night before baking the bread, combine all ingedients in a medium sized mixing bowl. Stir well to combine, but note ...the mixture will seem too dry and will not come together as a cohesive mass. This is OK!

Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight. Note: The flour will hydrate overnight...trust me!

Next morning: remove the bowl of dough from the refrigerator, setting it on the counter. Preheat your oven to its lowest temperature

Remove dough from the bowl (preserving plastic wrap) and knead it on the countertop a few times to bring everything together

Using your hands, shape the dough into a boule (don't know how? Google it!)

Turn off the oven once preheated, place the boule back into the (dirty) bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and place the bowl of dough in the over into warm the dough . Important: The oven is OFF, but warm at this point.

Wait a few hours for the dough to rise about double in the (turned off) oven.

Once the dough has approximately doubled, turn it out onto the countertop, flatten it out (removing large bubbles), and re-form the dough into a boule shape.

Place the boule, top-side down, into a prepared proofing basket

Spray a sheet of plastic cling wrap with cooking spray and cover the proofing basket (sprayed-side down)

Let the dough proof in the basket, on the countertop, until the basket is almost filled with the dough

Once the dough has risen...prepare the oven: Set an oven rack to the middle position, Set a pizza stone on the rack, Place a metal pie pan or other shallow metal pan on the bottom of the oven (for steam later), and Preheat the oven to 475° F

While the oven is preheating, remove plastic wrap from the proofing basket, sprinkle corn meal on the dough, and place a piece of parchment paper over the basket--generously overlapping its sides

Open the oven, slide out the rack with the stone, carefully invert the proofing basket (by holding it and the the parchment paper) to place the dough in the center of the stone

Slice the top of the dough about ½" deep in an "plus-sign" shape

Place a cup of icecubes into the metal pan (in the bottom of the hot oven)

Slide the rack into the oven, close the door, and bake for 10 minutes

After 10 minutes, lower the oven temperature to 425° F, and bake for 20 minutes more.

Remove the baked bread from the oven and place it on a rack to cool...the internal temperature should be around 195-200° F when done.
