Purpose

To provide marketing, information, and services to guests of the various Temple Inn & Suites locations

Audience

Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints who are temple patrons to their area's temple and those "temple tourists" who center travel around visiting Temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints.

This font has a more formal appearance, yet it still maintains good readability.

Paragraph Font: Lato

Good general sans serif font. Easy to read and general comapibilty with many system fonts (like Helvetica).
