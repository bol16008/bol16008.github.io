Normal paragraph example

Want that smell of bread in your home with minimal effort? Do you enjoy a bread with crust so durable you could soak it in the tub for a week and still hammer some tent pegs with it? Do you like to hear your bread talking to you when you pull it out of the oven? Wonder what to do with that 800 punds of wheat you bought before Y-2K?

Colored paragraph example

Naturally yeasted breads (A.K.A. sourdoughs) are healthier than store bought mass-produced breads. Sourdough takes on some of the unique qualities of your home, because the natural yeasts in each area vary slightly (sometimes significantly). Plus, since sourdoughs usually involve a slow-rise, they develop more flavor and temper some of the qualities which make people avoid wheat bread.
